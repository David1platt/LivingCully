Unique Local Gifts
curated by Inger McDowell-Hartye

Established in 2015, With Love, From PDX gift company offers elegantly curated custom gifts for weddings, events, and special occasions. The company sources locally- made goods and products from local businesses to design its gift collections. Our gifts are locally-inspired and elegantly curated. Inger McDowell-Hartye is the curator behind With Love, From PDX. She's combined both her passion for community and locally- made goods to grow With Love, From PDX into a successful company. Launched in 2015, our company curates gift collections with some of our city's most adored brands.
