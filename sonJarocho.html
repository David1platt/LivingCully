Colectivo de Son Jarocho
de Portland

El Colectivo de Son Jarocho is dedicated to learning, teaching and playing traditional Mexican music from the southern state of Veracruz.

We present often at community based events, fundraisers, music venues and other places. We also teach Son Jarocho every other Thursday at the Living Cully Plaza in NE Portland and organize "Fandangos" in coordination with other communities playing Son jJroch in Seattle, Salem and Eugene.
